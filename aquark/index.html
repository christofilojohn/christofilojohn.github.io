Who we Are?

AQUARK is an applied research & technology transfer SME that brings capacity to develop applied solutions as products and services in health management strategies and novel fish nutrition benchmarking tools by creating and managing networks in Mediterranean fish farming industry, the research community and the supply sector.

Established in Athens, Greece in 2004, AQUARK is a leading applied research firm in health and fish nutrition management, acting as technology and innovation amplifier in European Aquaculture.
